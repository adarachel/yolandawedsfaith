The Introduction

In February 2022, Faith and Yolanda were introduced through mutual friends. Faith's friend in Calgary and Yolanda's friend in England separately mentioned them to each other. Intrigued by the coincidence, Faith decided to learn more.

How it Began

Yolanda, visiting friends in Calgary, was told by her friend's husband about Faith. After a quick Instagram search, Yolanda discovered they were already mutual friends and shared connections in England. Eager to know more, Yolanda texted her English friend about Faith. To her surprise, she woke up the next morning to a text message from Faith.
